Transjakarta Data Analysis

This project aims to determine fare pricing based on travel distance for BRT buses, including a special scenario for peak hours, and compare Transjakartaâ€™s revenue between the old and new fare systems. It also analyzes the impact of fare changes on passengers based on age, gender, and payment type while implementing subsidies for students and seniors, along with a 10% discount on off-peak days. Lastly, the project evaluates passenger satisfaction before and after applying subsidies and discounts to determine the best scenario.
