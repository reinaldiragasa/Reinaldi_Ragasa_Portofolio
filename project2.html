CRUD for Oil and Gas Exploration

CRUD in oil and gas exploration helps determine well profitability by managing production and cost data. It records new well data (Create), retrieves oil, gas, and water production values along with prices and costs (Read), updates economic assumptions or production rates (Update), and removes outdated or irrelevant wells (Delete). This system ensures accurate financial analysis to decide whether drilling a well is worth the investment.
